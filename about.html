About Me

My Story

A journey through technology, innovation, and continuous learning across three continents

Who I Am

I am a seasoned software engineer with over a decade of experience building scalable, high-performance applications. My journey started as a Backend developer in Pakistan, where I honed my craft working on everything from store management systems to healthcare platforms.

My career took me to the UAE, where I spent years building enterprise event management platforms and virtual cycling applications that handled thousands of concurrent users. This experience taught me the importance of microservices architecture, real-time systems, and building for scale.

Now based in the UK, I'm currently at Verofy building next-generation payment infrastructure. I specialize in creating powerful abstractions that elegantly solve complex problems, with expertise spanning Laravel, Node.js, Vue.js, React, and increasingly AI/ML integration.

Philosophy

I believe great software is not just functional—it's elegant, maintainable, and empowering. Code should tell a story, architectures should evolve gracefully, and solutions should enable teams to move faster. My approach combines deep technical expertise with pragmatic decision-making, always keeping the end goal in sight while enjoying the journey of problem-solving.
